# Trader Behavior Insights using Market Sentiment

## 📌 Objective
Analyze the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear/Greed Index) and trader performance using historical trading data.

---

## 📊 Datasets Used
1. Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index
2. Historical Trader Data (Hyperliquid)

---

## ⚙️ Approach
- Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
- Merging datasets on date
- Feature Engineering (profit flag, trade size, sentiment score)
- Exploratory Data Analysis
- Visualization and Statistical Analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Traders achieve higher average profits during Greed phases.
- Win rate is slightly higher in Greed compared to Fear and Neutral.
- Fear periods show higher volatility with extreme gains and losses.
- Neutral sentiment leads to lower trading opportunities.
- Correlation between sentiment and PnL is very weak, indicating that sentiment alone does not drive profitability.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib / Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📌 Conclusion
Market sentiment influences trading behavior but does not solely determine success. Strategic execution and risk management play a more critical role in trader profitability.
